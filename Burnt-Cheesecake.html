Burnt Cheesecake

Introduction

Burnt cheesecake is a creamy and rich dessert that’s baked at a high temperature to give it a dark, caramelized top. It has no crust and a soft, smooth center. The burnt top adds a slightly bitter flavor that balances the sweetness.

This cheesecake is easy to make and perfect for anyone who loves a unique and tasty dessert.

Ingredients

  • 500g cream cheese
  • 200g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 200ml heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter. Press into a cake pan to form the base.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add vanilla and whipping cream. Mix until thick and creamy.
  4. Add vanilla and whipping cream. Mix until thick and creamy.
  5. Chill for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Top with fresh strawberries and optional jam before serving.
