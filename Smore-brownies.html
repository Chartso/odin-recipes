Smore Browines

Introduction

These delicious brownie s'mores let you enjoy the popular campfire treat on top of a brownie.

I consider myself a gourmet cook and originally thought these would just appeal to kids, but everyone loves them!

Ingredients

  • 1 package brownie mix
  • Oil, eggs, and water as directed on the brownie mix package
  • 6 graham crackers, broken into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 cups mini marshmallows
  • 1 cup chopped chocolate or chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Prepare the brownie batter according to the package instructions. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and bake for 15 minutes.
  3. While the brownies are baking, combine the broken graham crackers, mini marshmallows, and chopped chocolate in a bowl.
  4. After 15 minutes of baking, remove the brownies from the oven and sprinkle the graham cracker, marshmallow, and chocolate mixture evenly over the top.
  5. Return the pan to the oven and continue baking for an additional 10–15 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs.
  6. Allow the brownies to cool completely before cutting into squares and serving.
