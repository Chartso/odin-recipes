Strawberry Cheesecake

Introduction

Strawberry cheesecake is a classic dessert made with a creamy cheese filling on a crunchy biscuit base, topped with sweet and juicy strawberries. The combination of rich, smooth cheesecake and fresh fruit makes it a favorite for many.

It’s perfect for special occasions or just as a sweet treat anytime.

Crust

  • 1 ¼ cups graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup white sugar/li>
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon, or to taste

Filling

  • 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen sweetened sliced strawberries, thawed and drained
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water (optional)

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C). Grease a 9-inch springform pan.
  2. In a bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and cinnamon. Mix well and press onto the bottom of the prepared pan. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. In a blender, puree the thawed strawberries with cornstarch until smooth.
  4. Pour the strawberry mixture into a saucepan and bring to a boil over high heat. Boil and stir until the sauce is thick and shiny, abou. Set aside 1/3 cup of the sauce to cool. Cover and refrigerate the remaining sauce for serving.
  5. In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese until fluffy. Gradually add the sweetened condensed milk, lemon juice, and vanilla extract, mixing until smooth. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  6. Pour half of the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan. Drizzle the reserved 1/3 cup strawberry sauce over the filling. Top with the remaining cream cheese mixture.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour or until the center is set. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for 1 hour.
  8. Remove the cheesecake from the oven and cool to room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  9. Before serving, remove the sides of the springform pan and spoon the reserved strawberry sauce over the cheesecake.
